\# Cross-Sectional Momentum Research



\## Hypothesis



Assets that have outperformed their peers over the recent past will continue to outperform in the near future.



\## Methodology



A cross-sectional momentum strategy was developed using a universe of ETFs, equities, commodities, and other liquid assets. Assets were ranked based on their one-month momentum score. The top-ranked assets were selected and weighted using an inverse-volatility weighting scheme.



The portfolio was rebalanced monthly.



\## Results



The strategy generated positive risk-adjusted returns and outperformed SPY on a risk-adjusted basis. Walk-forward testing showed that the strategy remained robust across multiple market environments.



\## Conclusion



Cross-sectional momentum became the foundation of the production strategy due to its simplicity, robustness, and consistent performance across testing periods.
