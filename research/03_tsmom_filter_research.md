# TSMOM Filter Research



## Hypothesis



Removing assets with negative momentum may improve overall portfolio quality.



## Methodology



A positive momentum filter was applied before cross-sectional ranking. Multiple thresholds were tested, including 0%, 1%, 3%, and 5%.



## Results



Small positive thresholds produced modest improvements while larger thresholds reduced performance.



The 1% threshold produced the strongest overall results among the tested values.



## Conclusion



Moderate filtering may improve signal quality, but aggressive filtering reduces diversification and weakens performance.
